by Giahuy Pham

Instructions

Step 1:
  • Visit 'mybk' → 'đăng ký môn học'.
  • Seek your course.
Step 2:
  • Ctrl/ + A.
  • Ctrl/ + C.
Step 3:
  • Click 'Add Course' button.
  • Ctrl/ + V into the textbox.
Step 4:
  • Back to Step 1, supposing more courses are needed.
  • Otherwise, click 'Del Course' button.
Step 5:
  • Click 'Submit' button.
Note: Classes must be popped out, wherefore tool executes properly.
